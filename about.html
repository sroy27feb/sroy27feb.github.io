About Us

'Fal Sabji Bazar' as the name indicates is a vegetable cart, having traditional touch of conventional cart vendors and freshness and quality oriented approach of young minds. We aim to provide you fresh vegetables and fruits at your doorstep. There has been many of such portals offering same service but how often have you used them?

The reason why you still prefer to go out and buy vegetables and fruit, is that you know that no one else can select the best things better then you for yourself. In our 'Fal Sabji Bazar' the very factor of ‘touch and feel” exists. Now whenever you want you can call your own vegetable and fruit shop on your doorstep and select your own items.

We are not only limited to this, you can also find our carts roaming around the streets to fulfil your daily vegetable and fruits needs. You can also find our carts in your nearby shopping spots. Lately our carts will be enriched with more smartness, as we are making them GPS enabled, now you don’t have to search for a shop full of freshness.

For a change an entire shop will be chasing you with our GPS based technology till your home. We also believe in the concept of providing ‘cheap and the best’. Nobody is a better judge of things than you, while analysing something. Hence you can compare our prices with any vegetable and fruits vendors available in the market. We promise to provide you highest quality and freshness in cheapest rates. We are looking forward to provide you the fastest delivery by increasing the number of carts.
